Finance & Insurance

We have an unrivalled experience in advising Azerbaijan`s leading banks, leasing companies, investments funds, and other financial institutions on all aspects of finance and banks transactions with in-depth understanding of the financial regulatory requirements and potential legal obstacles to the operation of banks.

We assist through the investment process, from seeking and structuring debt or equity investment and undertaking pre-investment due diligence, to reviewing and negotiating investment or finance documentation.

Our dedicated team has solid knowledge of the insurance and reinsurance area. We have advised insurance and reinsurance companies on the regulatory and commersial issues of the insurance sector and and have provided professional legal services to resolve the most complex domestic and international insurance and reinsurance disputes.

Our attorneys have significant insights and longstanding experience in assisting industry specific regulatory and compliance matters, the sufficiency of our clients’ insurance coverage and indemnity arrangements to protect their personnel, financial resources, and property from unintended and unanticipated losses, risk management, development of insurance products and licensing, market entry and sale strategies, representation in disputes and enforcement, as well as other related services.
